About Us

The origins of the band can be traced back to Tom and Elliot’s school band in the small ex-mining town of Ystradgynlais in the South Wales Valleys in 2010. A band covering their favourite songs from Nirvana to Chuck Berry!

Following a brief pause, the band reformed in 2015 under the name of Serene Concept. The band started playing small venues, pubs and clubs as a cover band in and around the Swansea Valley and started writing their own songs.

A number line-up changes has taken place over the years, with Tom and Elliott ever present. The current members have clicked in a way that has sparked the success – a name change to Rapid Unscheduled Disassembly seemed right.

Having been the supporting act to a number of big names such as Stone Sour and Billy Talent in 2019.

The band signed their first record deal in January 2020 and are looking forward to touring their album when restrictions on live shows are lifted.
